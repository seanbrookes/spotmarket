About spotmarket

Spotmarket is a startup based in Mission, British Columbia in the beautiful (and fertile) Fraser Valley.

Traditionally a 'spot market' is whatever price a farmer can get for their goods at any given time based on traditional relationships or technologies i.e. phone and word of mouth.

Spotmarket aims to change all that by establishing a simple, user friendly platform for brewers, restauranteurs, and grocers to be more aware of what is available locally, who has grown it and how it is grown and processed.

Craigslist meets eBay  meets facebook for small-scale, regional growers.

For the end customer

There are converging movements, back to earth, buy local, climate change, software eating the world, emergence of the craft beer industry and consequent hop growing revivalist movement.

All of these combined make the case for an efficient way for people to promote produce locally as well as for people learn about and source food locally.

For the vendor (eg: brewers)

Customers are demanding local hops in their beers.

People are looking to lower their carbon footprint. 

Brewers are looking for information to make informed decisions when sourcing local hops.

For growers

Growers need a way to promote themselves and their hops

- Social media integration (facebook, twitter, and instagram feeds)

- Enhanced web presence (more potential customers)

- A community to collaborate with and learn from

The plan

There are viable market opportunities emerging around what would traditionally be called 'back to the earth' or 'buy local' movements making use of mobile / real-time / web technologies to communicate and conduct business far more efficiently and environmentally friendly than before.

Not just for Beer Hops

Spotmarket is targeting the Beer Hops market in British Columbia but the plan is much bigger.  As the business models become clearer we will expand to other craft beer / hop growing regions across Canada and the US (why not globally) 

While the beer hop market is exciting in and of itself it is not the only crop / market opportunity.  There are many other product types spotmarket can serve such as the herb market for restaurants, and local premium vegetable and fruit growers, etc.

Road map

Some ideas on the drawing board include: simple storefront and payment processing (think Shopify) as well as shipping and transport logistics (think Uber)

Business model

  • A work in progress (like any good startup)
  • Freemium based (business model is a tiered subscription / premium feature model tbd) - Basic market participation is free to avoid any friction (no membership sign up required up front).
  • Create a platform to build business models on. 
  • Premium features: payment processing / enhanced store front / dynamic inventory monitoring / brewers recipe wishlist / suplier relationship history, Value added market analytics/metrics collection and analytics will be the core of the value proposition.  user registration, usage tracking, pricing metrics will be collected and used to provide insight and market intelligence for a subscriber based revenue stream.
